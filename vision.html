My Vision

Ideally, I dream of a future where scientific progress and technological advancements triumph over divisions based on religion, caste, or personal classifications. In my vision, people of all ranks, statuses, and occupations collaborate passionately to create a society driven by science and technology. To me, science is the universal language that bridges divides, fostering a culture of mutual understanding and addressing the problems that hinder humanity's progress.

To achieve this vision, I recommend a coordinated, systematic strategy wherein coherent and well-supported science is embraced as the unifying thread throughout the social fabric. I kindly appeal to everyone to join this mission, making science the guiding principle of our world.

Together, we can strive for a world where scientific and technological advancements are not just processes but a way of life. As scientists, it is our duty to construct a world where people share a fundamental interest in science, paving the way for a harmonious and progressive society.
