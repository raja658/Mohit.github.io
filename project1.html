Cybertruck

We, a group of four friends, designed a cybertruck prototype with a remote-controlled system. Our design was inspired by the need for transportation on Mars, with the entire body of the vehicle covered in a metal sheet to protect it from the Martian environment.

Our future plans included adding thermal cameras and high-resolution normal vision cameras to provide a complete feed of the surroundings to the user inside and the control room. Although our design was based on Tesla's cybertruck, our version was more protective and completely remote-based, introducing a cyber world feel to the user inside.

After the final demonstration of the project, we gifted it to our teachers, but they didn't understand the concept of the car and thought it was a toy for a child. One of the teachers even took it home for her child. However, I believe that one day they will see the same model car running on Mars, directing humanity into a new era of space exploration.
