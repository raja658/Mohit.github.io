Our further plans include adding thermal cameras and high-resolution normal vision cameras to provide a complete feed of the surroundings to the user inside and the control room. Although our design is based on Tesla's cybertruck, our version is more protective and completely remote-based, introducing a cyber world feel to the user inside.

We envision a future where our cybertruck prototype plays a crucial role in humanity's exploration of Mars, providing a safe and efficient mode of transportation for astronauts and scientists.
